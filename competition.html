Please read the criteria by which we are evaluated in competition in the United States Scholastic Band Association. In 2018 new judges sheets were revised for A Class Bands. Each sheet has a front side and a back side which shows the rubric for scoring in levels of achievement, boxes 1 - 5. The scores for each of these boxes of achievement slide upward throughout the season every other week to ensure an elevation of score throughout the season.
