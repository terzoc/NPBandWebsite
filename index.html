Program Philosophy

The Pride & Class of New Providence, The New Providence High School Marching Band is a COMPLETE team activity where EVERYONE, their TIME and EFFORT are IMPORTANT. Although we participate as a competitive entity, the stress is not on a placement or score, but a complete dedication to excellence, character and improvement.

ABOVE ALL we work to make sure that our actions represent that of great people as we strive to be not only proficient performers but quality ambassadors of our school, community, families and of ourselves.
